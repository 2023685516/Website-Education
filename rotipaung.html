Roti Paung (Roti Butut)

Small, pull-apart buns that are incredibly soft and fluffy. Traditionally famous in Terengganu, these "Paung" buns are perfect when served warm with a cup of local coffee.

2 Hours (Dough)
Medium
20+ Pcs

Ingredients

Bahan Yis:

1 cawan air suam
1 paket yis mauripan
2 sudu makan gula
3 sudu makan susu pekat
1 biji telur (dipukul sedikit)

Bahan Tepung:

3 cawan tepung gandum
2 sudu makan marjerin
Secubit garam

WATCH: HOW TO MAKE ROTI PAUNG

Instructions

1
Activate the Yeast: Mix warm water, yeast, sugar, condensed milk, and beaten egg. Stir well and let it sit until it becomes frothy/activated.
2
Mix Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, combine wheat flour with margarine and salt. Mix until it reaches a breadcrumb-like texture.
3
Knead the Dough: Gradually pour the yeast mixture into the flour. Knead until the dough becomes smooth, elastic, and no longer sticks to your hands.
4
Proof & Shape: Cover the dough and let it proof until doubled in size. Punch down the dough to release air. Shape into small balls and arrange them closely in a greased baking tray.
5
Bake: Brush the top with full cream milk. Bake in a preheated oven at 180Â°C for about 25 minutes. Brush with margarine immediately after baking for a shiny finish.
