Laksa Kuah Merah

A vibrant and bold Terengganu delicacy. Unlike its white gravy counterpart, this version features a rich, red fish gravy infused with aromatics, kerisik, and a hint of spice.

45 Mins
Medium
6-8 Pax

Ingredients

Bahan Kuah Merah:

600g ikan (kembung/selayang) - rebus & kisar
1.5kg santan pekat
1-3 sudu besar cili boh
Bawang, garlic, ginger & lengkuas (kisar)
50g kerisik & gula melaka
Asam keping, garam & gula

Bahan Hidangan:

1kg mi laksa beras
Ulam: Kacang panjang, taugeh, bunga kantan & timun
Sambal belacan & limau kasturi

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prepare the Fish: Boil the fish until cooked. Remove the bones and skin, then flake or blend the fish meat until smooth. Keep the fish broth as it adds deep flavor to the gravy.
2
Sauté the Aromatics: Heat oil in a large pot. Sauté the blended onions, garlic, ginger, galangal, and chili paste until fragrant and the oil separates (pecah minyak).
3
Build the Gravy: Pour in the fish broth and water. Add coconut milk, tamarind slices, palm sugar, and kerisik. Bring to a gentle simmer, ensuring the coconut milk doesn't split.
4
Thicken and Season: Once simmering, stir in the ground fish meat. Season with salt and sugar. Continue to simmer until the gravy thickens slightly and develops a rich red hue.
5
Serve: Place a portion of laksa noodles in a bowl. Ladle the hot red gravy over the noodles and garnish with fresh ulam and a dollop of sambal belacan.
