Refreshing Island Treat

Teochew Chendul

A refreshing chilled dessert made of shaved ice, coconut milk, palm sugar, green rice flour jelly, and red beans. A must-have on a hot Penang afternoon.

30 Mins
Sweet
Dessert

The Base

Finely Shaved Ice
Fresh Coconut Milk (Santan)
Pinch of Salt

Star Components

Green Rice Flour Jelly (Cendol)
Cooked Kidney Beans
Pandan Juice

Sweetener

Gula Melaka (Palm Sugar) Syrup
Pandan Leaves

Watch Recipe Tutorial

How to assemble the perfect Penang Cendol

Preparation Steps

1
The Syrup: Boil gula melaka with water and pandan leaves until thick and syrupy. Strain and let it cool completely.
2
The Santan: Mix fresh coconut milk with a tiny pinch of salt to enhance the richness. Keep chilled until ready to use.
3
Shaving Ice: Use an ice shaver to create a fine, snow-like texture. Pack it tightly into a mound in the center of the bowl.
4
The Final Pour: Top the ice with green jelly strings and red beans. Pour over the coconut milk first, then finish with a generous drizzle of the dark Gula Melaka syrup.
