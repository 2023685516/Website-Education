Laksa Johor

The uniqueness of laksa in its use of spaghetti and rich, thick fish broth.

45 Minit
Sederhana
5 Hidangan

Ingredients

500g Spaghetti (boiled)
1kg Ikan Parang / Kembung
200ml Santan Pekat
3 Sudu Besar Kerisik
2 Batang Serai (diketuk)
Asam Gelugur
Daun Kesum & Bunga Kantan

Blended Ingredients:

10 Biji Bawang Merah
3 Ulas Bawang Putih
2 Inci Lengkuas & Halia

Step by Step

1
Prepare the Fish: Boil the fish with tamarind slices and salt. Remove the flesh and blend until smooth. Keep the fish broth for later use.
2
Stir-fry: Heat oil and stir-fry the blended ingredients with lemongrass until aromatic and oil separates.
3
Cook the Sauce: Add the blended fish, fish broth, and coconut milk. Stir gently to prevent the coconut milk from curdling.
4
Season: Add toasted coconut (kerisik), torch ginger flower, and laksa leaves. Cook until the sauce thickens, then season with salt and a little sugar.
