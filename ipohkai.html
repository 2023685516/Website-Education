Silky Smooth Ipoh Specialty

Ipoh Kai See Hor Fun

Thin, flat rice noodles served in a crystal-clear soup made from chicken bones and prawn shells. This light yet flavorful dish is topped with shredded chicken, prawns, and fresh chives.

50 Mins
Light
Traditional

Noodles & Broth

500g Thin Hor Fun (Rice Noodles)
Chicken Bones (from 1 whole chicken)
200g Prawn Shells & Heads
Rock Sugar & Salt

Toppings

Whole Chicken (boiled & shredded)
Large Prawns (peeled)
Fresh Chives (cut into lengths)
Fried Shallots

The Secret Oil

Prawn Oil (rendered from heads)

Watch Recipe Tutorial

Learn the authentic way to make the crystal-clear broth

Preparation Steps

1
The Broth: Simmer chicken bones in a large pot of water for 45 minutes. Skim the surface to keep the soup clear. Add the prawn shells (pre-fried for extra flavor) and simmer for another 15 minutes.
2
The Protein: Boil the whole chicken in the broth until cooked. Remove, let it cool, and shred the breast and thigh meat into thin strips. Blanch the prawns in the same broth until pink.
3
The Prawn Oil: Sauté prawn heads in oil until the oil turns a bright orange. Strain and set aside—this is the secret to the dish's distinct aroma.
4
Assembly: Blanch the hor fun briefly and place in a bowl. Arrange shredded chicken, prawns, and chives on top. Pour the hot clear broth over. Finish with a drizzle of prawn oil and a sprinkle of fried shallots.
