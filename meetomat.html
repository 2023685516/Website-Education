Mee Tomato

Stir-fried noodles in a savory tomato and oyster sauce, with chicken, vegetables, and optional seafood, flavored with garlic, shallots, and ginger.

25 Minutes
Easy
3-4 Servings

Ingredients

Yellow noodles
7 cloves garlic, finely chopped
5 shallots, finely chopped
2 cm ginger, thinly sliced
50 g chicken breast, diced
prawns & squid, cleaned
100 ml water
2 tomatoes, quartered
Fish cake, as desired
Bok choy, as desired
Tomato sauce (about 1 bottle)
3 tbsp oyster sauce
Sesame oil
Cornstarch slurry (3 tsp mixed with water)
Salt,sugar & seasoning

Preparation Steps

1
Heat sesame oil and blend-fry garlic, shallots, and ginger until golden.
2
Add chicken and cook through.
3
Pour in water and bring to a boil, then stir in tomato sauce, oyster sauce, and tomatoes.
4
Simmer for 5 minutes.
5
Add prawns, squid, and fish cake, then toss everything together.
6
Stir in bok choy and season with salt, sugar, and other seasonings to taste.
7
To thicken the sauce, mix in cornstarch slurry and cook for 2 minutes. Your tomato noodle sauce is ready!
