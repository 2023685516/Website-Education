Onde Onde

A traditional kuih filled with sweet Melaka sugar, spiced, layered with grated coconut, bursting with flavor in every bite.

25 Minutes
Intermediate
4-6 Servings

BAHAN JUS PANDAN

5-6 helai daun pandan
1 1/2 cawan air

BAHAN DOH & INTI

1 cawan tepung pulut
2 sudu besar tepung beras / gandum
1/4 sudu kecil pewarna hijau
Gula melaka / Gula nisan (potong kiub)

BAHAN SALUTAN

1/2 biji kelapa parut
Garam secukup rasa

How to Make Onde-Onde

Learn the right technique to make soft glutinous rice balls that stay perfectly intact.

Tonton Tutorial

COOKING STEPS

1
Cut the pandan leaves, blend with water, and strain to obtain fresh pandan juice.
2
In a bowl, mix the glutinous rice flour and rice/wheat flour.
3
Gradually pour the pandan juice into the flour mixture. Add green food coloring if necessary.
4
Knead until it becomes a soft dough that does not stick to your hands.
5
Prepare the filling by cutting the Palm Sugar (Gula Melaka) into small cubes.
6
Mix the grated coconut with a pinch of salt. Steam for a short while (5 minutes) so the coconut lasts longer.
7
Take a small amount of dough, roll it into a ball, and flatten it. Place a cube of Palm Sugar in the center, seal it tightly, and roll it back into a ball.
8
Bring water to a boil in a large pot. Use medium heat so the sugar inside has time to melt before the balls float.
9
Once the balls float, remove them and immediately roll them in the grated coconut while still hot. Ready to serve!
