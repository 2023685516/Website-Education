Mee Bandung Muar

Savory dried shrimp paired with a rich red sauce that delights the taste buds.

30 Minutes
Sederhana
5 Hidangan

Ingredients

1 camca besar sos tiram
2 bij telur
1 paket mee kuning
8-10 tangkai cili kering
3 ulas bawang merah
2 ulas bawang putih
2 sudu besar sos tiram
2 sudu besar udang kering
200g udang
200g daging
Garam dan gula secukup rasa
3 cawan stok daging
4-5 tangkai sawi
Sedikit kubis

Step by Step

1
Boil dried shrimp with 1/2 cup of water. Remove the flesh and blend until smooth. Keep the fish broth for later use.
2
Blend dried chili, garlic, shallots, and dried shrimp together with the boiled shrimp water.
3
Heat a little oil and saut√© the blended mixture until the oil separates.
4
Add mustard stems, oyster sauce, shrimp, and meat; stir briefly, then add 3 cups of meat broth.
5
Add mustard leaves and cabbage along with the cornstarch mixture.
6
Cook until boiling, then pour over the noodles.
