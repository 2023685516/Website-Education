Hinompuka

Sweet and fragrant steamed rice cake from Sabah made with coconut milk, palm sugar, and banana leaves.

40 Minutes
Medium
3-4 Servings

Ingredients

Glutinous rice flour
Coconut milk (santan)
Palm sugar (Gula Melaka)
Banana leaves
Grated cassava (optional)

Preparation Steps

1
Make the Batter: Mix glutinous rice flour with coconut milk and dissolved palm sugar until smooth
2
Wraps: Soften banana leaves, spoon in the batter, and fold neatly.
3
Steam: Steam for 30â€“40 minutes until the cake is chewy and no longer sticks to the leaf.
4
Serve: Enjoy warm or at room temperature, perfect with tea or coffee.
