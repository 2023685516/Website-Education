Silky Perak Custard

Kuih Limas

A traditional double-layered custard dessert from Perak. Made from a delicate blend of flour, rich coconut milk, and aromatic pandan juice, it is steamed to perfection in elegant banana leaf boats (perahu).

60 Mins
Sweet
Dessert

Bottom Layer (Green)

1 cup Rice Flour
1 cup Pandan Juice (from 5 leaves)
1 cup Sugar
3 cups Water
A drop of green coloring (optional)

Top Layer (White)

1/2 cup Rice Flour
3 cups Thick Coconut Milk
1 tsp Salt

Packaging

Banana Leaves (Softened)
Small Granulated Sugar (for sprinkling)

Watch Recipe Tutorial

Step-by-step guide to making authentic Kuih Limas

Preparation Steps

1
The Banana Boats: Cut banana leaves into rectangles. Fold and secure them into "perahu" or boat shapes using staples or toothpicks. Place a small amount of granulated sugar at the bottom of each boat.
2
Green Layer: Mix rice flour, pandan juice, sugar, and water in a pot. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and turns glossy. Pour into the banana boats until 3/4 full.
3
White Layer: Combine thick coconut milk, rice flour, and salt. Cook until slightly thickened and smooth. Carefully pour this over the green layer until the boats are full.
4
Steaming: Arrange the boats in a steamer. Steam for 10-15 minutes on medium heat. Let them cool completely before serving to allow the custard to set into its signature "gedik" (wobbly) texture.
