Gulai Patin Tempoyak

A traditional Malaysian curry where succulent Patin fish (Silver Catfish) is braised in a spicy, tangy gravy thickened with Tempoyak (fermented durian). A bold and soul-warming dish.

45 Mins
Medium
4 Pax

Ingredients

Bahan Utama:

2–4 keping ikan patin
200–300g tempoyak
900ml–1L air
3–5 biji cili padi (ikut pedas)

Bahan Kisar:

5–7 ulas bawang merah
3–4 ulas bawang putih
1–2 inci kunyit hidup

Aromatik & Perasa:

2–3 batang serai (diketuk)
1–2 keping asam keping
1 genggam daun kesum
Garam & Gula secukup rasa

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Make the Spice Base: Blend the shallots, garlic, and turmeric into a smooth paste. Add this paste to a medium pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
2
Add Tempoyak and Liquid: Stir the tempoyak into the pot with the spice paste. Pour in the water and bring it to a gentle simmer. Add the bruised lemongrass and asam keping.
3
Season: Let the curry base simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Season with salt and a touch of sugar to balance the tanginess of the fermented durian.
4
Cook the Fish: Gently lower the Patin fish pieces into the gravy. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the fish is tender and opaque.
5
Final Aroma: Toss in the torn daun kesum (Vietnamese coriander) during the last few minutes of cooking for that signature fragrance. Serve hot with white rice.
