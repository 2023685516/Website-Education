The Fiery Noodle Soup

Penang White Curry Mee

A unique noodle soup served with a base of pale coconut milk broth. It is transformed into a fiery red dish once the secret spicy chili paste is stirred in.

Step-by-step Penang Curry Mee Tutorial

Preparation Steps

1
The Stock: Boil prawn shells and heads with water for 45 minutes to create a sweet seafood base. Strain and add pandan leaves for aroma.
2
The Secret Sambal: Sauté the blended chili, belacan, and dried shrimp in plenty of oil until fragrant and the oil separates (pecah minyak). This paste is the heart of the dish!
3
The White Broth: Add coconut milk to the prawn stock. Season with salt and sugar. Keep it on a low simmer—the broth should stay pale/white at this stage.
4
Serving: Blanch the noodles and place in a bowl. Top with cockles, cuttlefish, and tofu puffs. Pour the hot white broth over. Serve with a spoonful of the spicy sambal on the side or on top to stir in.
