Gulai Ketam

A rich curry infused with aromatic spices and the natural sweetness of fresh crabs, perfected with the "Tumis Darat" technique (final tempering) to awaken the senses.

45 Mins
Medium
4 Servings

Main Ingredients

5 Crabs (cleaned & halved)
2 packets Crab Curry Powder
1/2 Pineapple (sliced)
1/2 bowl Thick Coconut Milk
Tamarind Juice (to taste)

Tempering (Tumis Darat)

1 Red Onion (sliced)
3 cloves Garlic (sliced)
1 inch Ginger (julienned)
3 sprigs Curry Leaves
1 tsp Mixed Halba (Spices)

Watch Full Tutorial

Northern Style Crab Curry Tutorial

Preparation Steps

1
Prepare Curry Paste: Mix the curry powder with a little water to form a thick paste. Blend the onions, garlic, and ginger until smooth.
2
Simmer Curry: Combine the blended aromatics, curry paste, crabs, and a bit of water in a pot. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes until the spices are well-cooked.
3
Flavoring: Add the pineapple slices, coconut milk, sugar, salt, and tamarind juice. Bring to a gentle boil.
4
Tempering (Tumis Darat): In a separate small pan, fry the sliced onions, garlic, ginger, curry leaves, and halba until golden and fragrant. Pour the hot oil and fried ingredients directly into the curry pot. Ready to serve!
