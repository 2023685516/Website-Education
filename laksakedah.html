Laksa Kedah

An authentic Northern Malaysian favorite. Thick rice noodles served in a rich, tangy mackerel broth with fresh local herbs.

85 Mins
Hard
6 Servings

Ingredients

1kg Rice Noodles
1kg Mackerel (Kembung)
5-6 Tamarind Peels
Daun Kesum & Bunga Kantan
2 tbsp Shrimp Paste (Belacan)
Petis Udang (Otak Udang)

Preparation Steps

1
Fish Prep: Boil fish with tamarind and salt. Blend the flesh. Blend bones separately and strain to get the stock.
2
The Gravy: Blend onions, chilies, and belacan. Combine with fish paste and stock in a large pot.
3
Simmer: Add daun kesum, bunga kantan, and petis udang. Simmer for 45 minutes until the broth is rich.
4
Serve: Pour the hot gravy over noodles and garnish with cucumber, eggs, and lime.
