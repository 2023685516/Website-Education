Ikan Rebus Berlada

A simple yet flavorful dish featuring boiled fish sautéed in a coarse chili and lemongrass spice paste. Perfect with warm rice!

30 Mins
Easy
2 Servings

Main Ingredients

2 Boiled Mackerel (Ikan Rebus)

Spice Paste (Coarsely Ground)

1 stalk Lemongrass
1 inch Shrimp Paste (Belacan)
2 cloves Garlic
5 small Red Onions
2 pieces Dried Tamarind (Asam Keping)
2 large Red Chilies
6 Bird's Eye Chilies (Cili Padi)

Watch Video Tutorial

Step-by-step tutorial for Ikan Rebus Berlada

Preparation steps

1
Grind Spices: Coarsely grind the lemongrass, shrimp paste, garlic, red onions, red chilies, and bird's eye chilies using a mortar and pestle [00:01:05].
2
Fry Fish: Fry the boiled mackerel in a pan until lightly golden [00:01:20]. Remove and set aside.
3
Sauté Paste: Using the same oil, sauté the coarsely ground spice paste until it becomes dry and slightly crispy [00:01:55].
4
Season and Combine: Add salt and sugar to taste [00:02:16]. Mix well. Add the fried mackerel and stir gently to combine, being careful not to break the fish [00:02:40]. Turn off the heat and serve!
