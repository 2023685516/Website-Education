Resepi Warisan Kelantan

Lompat Tikam

A beloved colorful kuih featuring a silky green pandan layer, a creamy white coconut layer, and vibrant red glutinous rice, all brought together by a rich palm sugar syrup.

45 Mins
Medium
6-10 Pax

Ingredients

Lapisan Hijau:

1 cawan tepung beras
3 cawan air pandan
Sedikit garam & air kapur

Lapisan Putih:

2 cawan santan
3 sudu besar tepung beras
Garam secukup rasa

Pulut Merah:

1 cawan beras pulut
Pewarna merah & santan

Air Gula Melaka:

300g gula Melaka
2 cawan air & daun pandan

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prepare the Red Glutinous Rice: Soak the rice with red coloring for 30 minutes. Drain and steam with a little coconut milk, water, and salt until tender. Set aside to cool.
2
Make the Palm Sugar Syrup: Combine gula Melaka, water, and a knotted pandan leaf in a pot. Simmer until the sugar dissolves and the syrup thickens slightly. Strain and let it cool.
3
Cook the Green Pandan Layer: Mix rice flour, pandan juice, and salt. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until the mixture becomes thick, glossy, and translucent. Pour into a tray and steam for 15 minutes to set.
4
Cook the White Coconut Layer: Whisk coconut milk, rice flour, and salt together. Stir over low heat until creamy and thick. Pour this over the set green layer or keep it separate to scoop later.
5
Assemble & Serve: Scoop a portion of the green and white layers into a bowl. Add a spoonful of red glutinous rice and drizzle generously with the palm sugar syrup. Best served chilled!
