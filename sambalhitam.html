Sambal Hitam Pahang

A legendary spicy-sour paste from Pahang. Its signature dark color comes from hours of slow-cooking belimbing buluh until it caramelizes into a deep, rich black hue.

4 Hours
Patience
1 Jar

Ingredients

Bahan Utama:

500g belimbing buluh
15–20 biji cili padi
1 genggam ikan bilis
3–5 ulas bawang merah
Garam & Gula secukup rasa
Minyak masak

WATCH: THE ART OF SAMBAL HITAM

Instructions

1
Boil Belimbing Buluh: Place cleaned belimbing buluh in a pot without water. Cook over low heat for 2–4 hours until the fruit shrinks and turns dark brown/black.
2
Prepare Paste: Coarsely pound or blend the chillies, anchovies, and onions. Keep the texture chunky for a better mouthfeel.
3
Sauté: Heat oil and sauté the pounded mixture until fragrant and golden brown.
4
Combine: Add the darkened belimbing buluh into the pan. Mix well and season with salt and sugar to balance the acidity.
5
Cook to Perfection: Continue stirring until the sambal becomes thick, dry, and glossy. Let it cool before storing in a jar.
