Cek Mek Molek

A classic sweet potato fritter with a surprise molten sugar center. Literally translated as "Lovely Little Lady," this kuih is crispy on the outside and syrupy on the inside.

45 Mins
Easy
15 Pieces

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prepare the Sweet Potato: Peel, dice, and boil the sweet potatoes until tender. Drain the water and mash them thoroughly with a pinch of salt while still hot until smooth.
2
Make the Dough: Gradually add wheat flour to the mashed sweet potato. Knead gently until you get a soft, non-sticky dough. Avoid adding too much flour to keep the texture soft.
3
Shape & Fill: Take a small portion of dough (~size of a ping pong ball), flatten it, and place 1 teaspoon of sugar in the center. Carefully seal and shape it into a smooth, elongated oval.
4
Deep Fry: Heat oil over medium heat. Fry the kuih until golden brown and crispy. Be careful when taking the first bite as the melted sugar inside is very hot!
