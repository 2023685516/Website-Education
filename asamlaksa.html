Penang Asam Laksa

A sour and spicy fish-based noodle soup. The soul of this dish is the thick broth made from mackerel, torch ginger flower, and shrimp paste (Hae Ko).

95 Mins
Medium
4 Servings

Main Ingredients

1kg Fresh Rice Noodles (Laksa)
1kg Chub Mackerel (Ikan Kembung)
10 pcs Dried Tamarind (Asam Gelugur)
Shrimp Paste (Hae Ko)

Broth Spices & Herbs

Torch Ginger Flower (Bunga Kantan)
Vietnamese Coriander (Daun Kesum)
Chili Paste & Shrimp Paste (Belacan)

Garnishings

Sliced Cucumber & Pineapple
Red Onion & Bird's Eye Chili
Fresh Mint Leaves

Preparation Steps

1
Prepare the Broth: Boil the mackerel with dried tamarind. Once cooked, flake the fish meat and blend it coarsely. Save the boiling water as the base stock.
2
Simmer Spices: Add the blended chili paste, torch ginger flower, Vietnamese coriander, and fish meat into the stock. Let it simmer for at least 45 minutes to develop deep flavors.
3
The Assembly: Blanch the rice noodles and place them in a bowl. Pour the hot, tangy broth over the noodles.
4
Finishing: Top with fresh cucumber, pineapple, onions, and mint. Finally, drizzle a generous spoonful of thick shrimp paste (Hae Ko) on top before serving.
