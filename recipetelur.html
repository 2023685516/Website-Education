Telur Pindang

A traditional Malay dish slow-cooked with a blend of spices and herbs, resulting in a dark brown color and fragrant aroma.

2 Days+
Intermediate
4-5 Servings

RAMUAN TELUR PINDANG

1 papan telur (30 biji)
1 sb ketumbar / jintan manis / putih
Rempah 4 beradik
2 botol kicap
Daun Manggis / Mengkudu / Jambu Batu
Daun Senduduk / Serai Wangi / Pandan
5 batang daun kari & 6 batang serai
10 biji bawang merah (belah, ada kulit)
3 inci lengkuas (hiris)
Air (paras tenggelam telur)

HOW TO COOK TELUR PINDANG

1
Clean the eggs and all the herb leaves.
2
Place the spices into a pot, then arrange the herb leaves on top.
3
Carefully add the eggs. Pour in soy sauce and water until the eggs are fully submerged.
4
Simmer over very low heat. Once the eggs start to firm up, transfer them to a rice cooker (keep-warm mode) for 2 nights.
