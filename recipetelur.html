Lempeng and Sambal Bilis

Lempeng with anchovy sambal is a simple traditional dish, yet packed with spicy and savory flavors.

30 Minutes
Easy - Medium

PANCAKE INGREDIENTS

3 cups wheat flour
4 - 4 1/2 cups water
2 tbsp cooking oil
1 tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt

SAMBAL INGREDIENTS

150g-200g anchovies
1 cup cooking oil
2 large onions
15-20 dried chilies
4 shallots & 3 cloves garlic
1/2 inch shrimp paste (belacan)
1 piece palm sugar & tamarind juice

Step by Step

HOW TO PREPARE LEMPENG

1
Mix wheat flour, salt, water, sugar, and cooking oil until well combined.
2
Strain the batter if there are any flour lumps to ensure a smooth texture.
3
Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it with a little oil.
4
Pour 2 ladles of batter, spread it evenly, and cook until golden brown on both sides.

HOW TO PREPARE SAMBAL BILIS

1
Blend dried chilies, shallots, garlic, and shrimp paste (belacan).
2
Saut√© the blended ingredients until the oil separates. Add palm sugar and tamarind juice.
3
Add the anchovies and season with salt to taste. Cook until the sambal reaches a beautiful consistency.
