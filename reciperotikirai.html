Roti kirai

Soft and sweet Roti Kirai, delicious on its own or dipped with your favorite filling.

30 Minutes
Easy-Medium
2-3 servings

BAHAN ROTI JALA

2 cawan tepung gandum
2 biji telur
1 1/2 cawan air
1 cwn susu cair / santan sederhana pekat
6 sb / 1/2 cawan mentega cair
1 sk garam & 1 sk serbuk kunyit

HOW TO PREPARE ROTI JALA

1
Mix the flour with milk, salt, melted butter, and eggs until well combined, then pour in the water. Blend or strain the batter to ensure there are no lumps.
2
Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it with a little oil.
3
Drizzle the batter onto the pan in a net-like pattern. Once the bread looks yellowish and set, remove it and roll it up neatly.
