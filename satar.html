Satar Ikan

A traditional grilled fish cake that captures the essence of Terengganu’s coastline. Made with seasoned fish paste and grated coconut, wrapped in banana leaves for a distinct smoky aroma.

40 Mins
Medium
12-15 Pcs

Ingredients

Bahan Utama:

1kg isi ikan (kembung/selayang/tenggiri)
1 cawan kelapa parut (muda)
1 biji telur & Daun kesum (dihiris halus)
Garam, gula & sedikit air asam jawa

Bahan Kisar:

10-30 biji cili padi (ikut pedas)
5-6 biji bawang merah & 3 ulas bawang putih
2 inci halia & 2 batang serai

Lain-lain:

Daun pisang (layur dahulu)
Lidi atau pencungkil gigi

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prepare the Fish: Briefly boil the fresh fish until just cooked. Remove the bones and skin, keeping only the flesh. Pound or process into a coarse paste.
2
Make the Paste: Blend the chilies, shallots, garlic, ginger, and lemongrass into a coarse paste. Mix this thoroughly with the prepared fish flesh.
3
Mix Ingredients: Add grated coconut, sliced daun kesum, salt, sugar, egg, and tamarind juice. Stir until the mixture is well-combined and aromatic.
4
Wrapping: Place a spoonful of the mixture on a piece of softened banana leaf. Shape into a small pyramid and secure with a toothpick.
5
Grill to Perfection: Grill the satar over medium heat for 10–15 minutes, turning regularly until the banana leaves are charred and the filling is cooked through and smoky.
