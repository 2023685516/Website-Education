Kuih Celorot

A traditional Sarawak snack wrapped in nipah leaves with a soft jelly-like brown filling.

60 Minutes
Hard
4-5 Servings

Ingredients

50 g rice flour/span>
100 g palm sugar (apong), chopped
300 ml coconut milk
50 ml water
A pinch of salt
Palm or coconut leaves, for wrapping

Preparation Steps

1
Prepare the wrappers: Cut palm leaves and fold into cone shapes, securing the ends.
2
Mix the batter: Combine rice flour, sugar (if not melted), and a little water until smooth.
3
Cook the batter: Add coconut milk and remaining water, stirring over low heat until thick and sticky (should coat the spoon). Do not burn.
4
Fill the cones: Spoon the thick batter into the leaf cones.
5
Steam: Arrange in a steamer and steam for 30â€“60 minutes, until set and firm
6
Serve: Let cool slightly before unwrapping and serving.
