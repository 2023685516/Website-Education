Kuih Sopang

Kuih Sopang is a traditional Negeri Sembilan dessert that is sweet and chewy, made from rice flour and grated coconut.

30 Minutes
Intermediate
4-6 Servings

ADUNAN TEPUNG

1 peket tepung pulut
Air secukupnya
Garam (secukup rasa)
Sedikit tepung beras
1 biji kelapa (santan)

BAHAN INTI

1 biji kelapa parut
3-4 ketul gula anau/kabung
3 helai daun pandan

How to make Kuih Sopang

Watch the proper technique for folding and steaming Kuih Sopang to get that perfect creamy texture.

Watch Tutorial

HOW TO PREPARE THE FILLING (INTI)

1
Place the shredded coconut into a pot. Add 3-4 pieces of palm sugar (gula anau/kabung) according to your preferred sweetness.
2
Add 3 knotted pandan leaves. Stir all ingredients well and cook over medium heat until the sugar dissolves and the coconut becomes slightly dry (ensure it is not too wet). Once done, set aside.

HOW TO PREPARE THE DOUGH & ASSEMBLY

3
Combine glutinous rice flour with water, adding it gradually while mixing. Add salt to taste and stir until the dough is smooth and well combined.
4
To shape the kuih, take a small portion of dough and roll it into a ball. Use your hands (lined with a banana leaf) to flatten the dough.
5
Place a portion of the prepared coconut filling in the center and fold the dough over, shaping it like a curry puff (karipap).
6
Arrange the shaped Kuih Sopang on a tray. Pour a mixture of coconut milk and a little rice flour over the kuih.
7
Steam the Kuih Sopang for 10 minutes until fully cooked. Serve warm.
