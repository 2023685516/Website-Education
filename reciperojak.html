Rojak Sotong

Rojak Sotong is a fresh and spicy dish with squid, vegetables, fruits, and a delicious sweet-spicy sauce.

25 Minutes
Intermediate
4-5 Servings

Ingredients

1 ekor sotong kembang (dicuci bersih)
1 ikat kangkung muda (dicuci bersih)
Bijan panggang (goreng tanpa minyak)

BAHAN SOS

2 ulas bawang putih (tumbuk halus)
2 sudu cili giling
4 sudu makan petis udang
2 sudu makan sos hoisin
2 sudu makan sos plum
1 sudu makan sos tiram
1 sudu makan kicap pekat
Gula secukup rasa
3 cawan air
Bancuhan tepung jagung (jika perlu)

How to Make Sotong Kangkung

Learn how to prepare this classic street food with a perfectly balanced sweet, savory, and spicy shrimp paste sauce.

Watch Tutorial

COOKING STEPS

1
Bring water to a boil and blanch the water spinach (kangkung) until soft. Then, boil the sliced cuttlefish (sotong kembang) for 10-15 minutes. Set both aside.
2
To prepare the sauce: Boil water in a pot, then add the chili paste (cili giling) and crushed garlic. Bring to a boil again.
3
Add the remaining sauce ingredients: shrimp paste (petis), hoisin sauce, plum sauce, oyster sauce, and thick soy sauce. Stir well and add sugar to taste.
4
Simmer the sauce until it thickens. If it is too thin, stir in a small amount of cornstarch mixture to reach the desired consistency.
5
Arrange the blanched kangkung and sliced cuttlefish on a plate, then pour the hot sauce generously over them.
6
Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds or finely crushed roasted peanuts for added texture and aroma.
7
Your delicious Sotong Kangkung is now ready to be enjoyed!
