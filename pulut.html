Pulut Harum Manis

A seasonal delicacy from Kedah, combining rich coconut glutinous rice with the sweet, fragrant Harum Manis mango.

35 Mins
Easy
4 Servings

Ingredients

500g Glutinous Rice
2 Harum Manis Mangoes
1 cup Thick Coconut Milk
1 tsp Salt
2 tbsp Sugar
2 Pandan Leaves

Preparation Steps

1
Steam the soaked glutinous rice with pandan leaves for 20 minutes.
2
Mix coconut milk, salt, and sugar. Pour over the hot rice and stir well.
3
Peel and slice the fresh Harum Manis mangoes.
4
Serve the rice and mango together. Drizzle with coconut sauce.
