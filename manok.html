Manok Pansuh

Tender chicken slow-cooked in bamboo with local herbs and spices for a fragrant smoky dish.

60 Minutes
Hard
4-5 Servings

Ingredients

1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
1 large onion, sliced
7 cloves garlic, sliced
2-inch ginger, sliced
1-inch galangal, sliced
3 stalks lemongrass, sliced
1 bunga kantan, sliced
2 bunches cassava shoots
Salt
2 bamboo shoots
