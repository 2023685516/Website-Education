Etok Salai

A unique East Coast delicacy consisting of river clams (Etok) marinated in aromatics and smoked over a slow fire. Itâ€™s smoky, savory, and incredibly addictive as a snack.

2 Hours
Smoking
1kg Clams

Inredients

1 kg etok segar (dicuci bersih)
2-3 batang serai (diketuk)
3 ulas bawang putih (ditumbuk)
3 ulas bawang merah (dimayang)
1 hiris halia (pilihan)
Garam & gula secukup rasa
Cili kering atau lada hitam (pilihan)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Clean the Etok: Rinse the clams thoroughly under running water multiple times. Scrub away any mud or sand until the water runs clear. This is crucial for a grit-free experience.
2
Prepare the Marinade: Pound the garlic, shallots, and ginger into a coarse paste. Toss the cleaned etok in a large bowl with the paste, bruised lemongrass, salt, and a pinch of sugar. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
3
Set Up the Smoker: Prepare a low-heat fire using charcoal or wood. For an authentic aroma, you can add coconut husks to the fire to create a thicker, fragrant smoke.
4
Smoking Process: Spread the etok evenly on a grill rack or bamboo platform. Smoke them for about 1 to 1.5 hours. Stir or flip them occasionally so the heat and smoke penetrate all the shells.
5
Serve: Once the clams are cooked through and infused with smoke, serve them warm. They are perfect as a snack to enjoy while chatting with friends and family.
