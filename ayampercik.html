Ayam Percik

A traditional Malaysian grilled chicken basted with a rich, creamy, and aromatic spiced coconut milk gravy. A true icon of East Coast cuisine.

60 Mins
Medium
6 Servings

Ingredients

Untuk Ayam:

1 ekor ayam (dipotong ikut kesukaan)
3 batang serai (dihiris halus)
2 inci halia atau lengkuas
Garam & gula secukup rasa

Bahan Kisar (Pes):

5-7 biji bawang merah
4 ulas bawang putih
4 biji cili merah (tambah jika nak pedas)
2 batang serai (bahagian putih sahaja)

Kuah Percik (Santan):

2 cawan santan pekat
1 sudu besar pes asam jawa
Gula Melaka / Nisan secukup rasa

Step-by-step Instructions

1
Prepare & Marinate: Clean the chicken and pat dry. Rub with salt, sugar, and a bit of turmeric. Set aside for at least 15 minutes.
2
Make Spice Paste: Blend shallots, garlic, chillies, and lemongrass into a smooth paste. Add a little oil if necessary to help the blending process.
3
Cook Coconut Sauce: Sauté the spice paste until fragrant. Add coconut milk, bruised lemongrass, tamarind, and sugar. Simmer on low heat for 10-15 minutes until thickened.
4
Grill & Baste: Grill the chicken (BBQ or Oven at 200°C). Brush the chicken repeatedly with the coconut sauce every 10 minutes to create a thick, caramelised glaze.
5
Serve: Once cooked and charred perfectly, drizzle extra gravy over the chicken. Serve hot with Nasi Kerabu or steamed white rice.
