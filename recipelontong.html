Lontong Kering

Lontong Kering is a traditional Johor dish featuring diced rice cakes mixed with thick peanut sauce, spicy sambal, coconut floss, bean sprouts, and sliced cucumber.

30 Minutes
Sederhana
10-15 Hidangan

SERUNDING KELAPA

50 tangkai cili kering
5 inci halia
15 batang serai
3 inci lengkuas
3 cawan udang kering
5 cawan ikan bilis
14 ulas bawang putih
25 ulas bawang merah
2 inc belacan
2 inc kunyit
Minyak masak untuk menumis
2 cawan air asam jawa
Garam dan gula secukup rasa
5 biji kelapa parut (ambil putih sahaja)

BAHAN DAGING DENDENG

5 kg daging (hiris)
2 mangkuk kicap (untuk perap)
Minyak masak secukupnya
15 ulas bawang putih (kisar)
20 ulas bawang merah (kisar)
5 inci halia (kisar)
2 inci lengkuas (kisar)
15 batang serai (kisar)
15 senduk besar lada (kisar)
10 senduk kicap lemak manis

SAMBAL KACANG TANAH DENGAN IKAN BILIS

Minyak masak untuk menumis
80 tangkai cili kering (kisar)
25 ulas bawang merah (kisar)
10 ulas bawang putih (kisar)
2 sudu kecil serbuk pati ayam
Garam secukup rasa
20 sudu kecil gula perang
1 kg kacang tanah (goreng)
1 kg ikan bilis (goreng)

HOW TO MAKE SERUNDING KELAPA

1
Blend all the ingredients above except the grated coconut, tamarind water, and sugar.
2
Heat cooking oil. Sauté the blended ingredients until fragrant and crispy.
3
Add tamarind water, salt, and sugar. Cook until the mixture thickens.
4
Once dry and crispy, add the grated coconut. Cook until dry and crumbly.

HOW TO MAKE DAGING DENDENG

1
Marinate the beef with soy sauce.
2
Heat cooking oil. Sauté the blended ingredients until fragrant and crispy..
3
Add the beef. Reduce the heat and cook until done, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
4
Add more soy sauce and stir well.

HOW TO MAKE SAMBAL KACANG TANAH DENGAN IKAN BILIS

1
Heat cooking oil. Sauté the blended ingredients.
2
Add chicken stock powder, salt, and palm sugar.
3
Let it simmer briefly until the sambal thickens and dries slightly.
4
Add peanuts and anchovies. Mix well, then turn off the heat.
