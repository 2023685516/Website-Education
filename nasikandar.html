Penang Nasi Kandar

An iconic Malaysian dish of steamed rice served with a variety of rich curries and side dishes. The secret lies in the "Kuah Banjir"â€”a mix of multiple curry gravies.

Learn the secret of Penang Nasi Kandar Curries

Preparation Steps

1
The Rice: Steam the rice with pandan leaves and a pinch of salt to create a fragrant base.
2
The Curries: Nasi Kandar is unique because it uses multiple curries. Prepare at least three types (Chicken, Fish, and Dhal) separately using authentic Indian-Muslim spices.
3
The Proteins: Marinate chicken with ginger, garlic, and turmeric before deep-frying. Prepare spicy honey beef or squid curry as additional sides.
4
Kuah Banjir: To serve, plate the rice and pour a little bit of every curry gravy over the rice until it is "flooded" (banjir). Add your sides and enjoy!
