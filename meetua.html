Mee Tuaran

A traditional noodle dish from Tuaran Sabah with springy noodles fried until crispy.

50 Minutes
Medium
2 Servings

Ingredients

Hakka Egg Roll:

2 eggs
200g minced chicken
1 tsp chicken powder
½ tsp pepper
½ tsp tapioca/cornstarch
1 tsp sesame oil (optional)
1 TBSP oil

Chicken Char Siew:

250g chicken thigh fillets
¼ cup hoisin sauce
3 TBSP sugar
1 TBSP light soy sauce
3 TBSP sugar
Red food colouring & white pepper
1 cup water

Noodles & Stir-Fry:

200g wonton noodles
2 cups water
1 egg
2 cloves garlic, minced
½ tsp sugar & pepper
Dash of soy sauce
½–1 cup Chinese greens
Oil for frying

Preparation Steps

1
Egg Roll: Beat eggs, cook thin omelettes, remove.
2
Mix minced chicken with seasoning until sticky.
3
Spread mince over omelette, roll up, steam 20 min, cool, cut 1cm slices.
1
Char Siew: Simmer chicken with sauce ingredients until cooked & caramelised.
2
Optional: blowtorch/grill for roasted effect. Cool and slice.
1
Noodles: Deep-fry noodles until crispy, remove, leave 1 TBSP oil.
2
Saute garlic, add egg and scramble.
3
Add noodles, stock, seasonings, simmer until reduced.
4
Add greens at the last 20 seconds, serve with egg roll & char siew.
